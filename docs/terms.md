# Terms of Service — ShopeePipeline

**Last updated: 2026-06-20**

ShopeePipeline is an automated affiliate video pipeline that publishes product review videos to TikTok on behalf of the account owner.

## Usage

This application is a personal automation tool. It generates short product videos using AI services and posts them to a single TikTok account owned and operated by the developer.

## TikTok Integration

By authorising this app, you allow ShopeePipeline to publish videos to your TikTok account using the Content Posting API. You may revoke access at any time via TikTok's connected-apps settings.
